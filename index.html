Competition Factory

Tournament Management Tools

Get Started

Standards based

The Competition Factory consumes, produces, and generates ITF standard TODS documents (a JSON format) ensuring that all mutations are valid.

Proven in production

Based on years of experience running thousands of events for governing bodies, Competition Factory now powers the tournament management platform of the USTA and the Intercollegiate Tennis Association.

Rigorously tested

Built following a Test Driven Development process utilizing Jest. With 350+ test suites and more than 1200 total tests covering greater than 92% of the code base, quality control is assured.
